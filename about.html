About Veil.ke

Where elegance meets faith — a Kenyan brand dedicated to redefining modest fashion.

Est. 2024 · Nairobi, Kenya

Elegance in Modesty

Veil.ke was born from a simple yet powerful belief: that a woman should never have to choose between her faith and her fashion. We set out to create abayas that are as elegant as they are modest, as contemporary as they are timeless.

Founded in Nairobi, Kenya, we serve women across East Africa who appreciate quality, craftsmanship, and design. Every Veil.ke abaya is thoughtfully selected for its premium fabric, impeccable tailoring, and flattering silhouette.

We work directly with trusted manufacturers who share our commitment to ethical production and quality materials. From the initial design concept to the final stitch, each piece reflects our dedication to excellence.

Our name, Veil.ke, represents both the beauty of the veil and our Kenyan roots. The ".ke" domain connects us to our home country and the vibrant community of Muslim women we serve.
