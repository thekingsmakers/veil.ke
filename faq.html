Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, shipping, and caring for your Veil.ke abaya.

Simply browse our collection, select the abaya you love, and contact us via WhatsApp or email to place your order. We'll guide you through sizing, payment, and delivery options.

Yes! We ship to Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, Burundi, South Sudan, and other East African countries. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

Our abayas are available in sizes S through 3XL. We recommend checking our size guide for precise measurements. If you need a custom size, please contact us and we'll be happy to help.

We accept M-Pesa, bank transfers, and mobile money for East African customers. International payments can be made via PayPal or bank transfer.

Delivery within Nairobi takes 1–3 business days. Upcountry Kenya takes 3–5 business days. East African deliveries take 5–10 business days depending on location.

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Custom-sized items are non-refundable.

To keep your abaya looking its best, we recommend dry cleaning for premium fabrics. For regular care, hand wash in cold water, hang to dry in shade, and iron on low heat. Avoid bleach and harsh detergents.
