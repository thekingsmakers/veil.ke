Our Story

Where Elegance Meets Faith

Veil.ke was born from a simple belief: that modesty and luxury can coexist beautifully. Founded in Kenya, we set out to create abayas that celebrate both faith and fashion.

Every piece in our collection is thoughtfully designed with premium fabrics, clean lines, and timeless silhouettes that flatter every body type. We source the finest materials from around the world, bringing you abayas that feel as beautiful as they look.
